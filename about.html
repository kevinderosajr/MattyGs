Here's how it all began

Every great story starts somewhere, and for us, it was in Murrieta, California. My Dad and Grandpa instilled in me a love for the game of baseball, and each weekend we gathered around the T.V. to catch the ballgame. The best part was listening to my Grandpa reminisce about "Hammerin" Hank Greenberg and my Dad talk about Sandy Koufax and Mickey Mantle. After the game we would head out to the park to play catch, then we would go to our favorite burger joint to enjoy a burger, fries, and a shake. Great memories!

As I grew up and progressed through college, I worked at local restaurants as a server and bartender, which grew my passion to someday open my own shop. 2016 was the year that happened, and we haven't looked back since.

At Matty G's we believe in two things - a great time and a great burger. Whether you're catching up with some friends, creating new memories with your family, or just stopping by to watch the ballgame - we're on a mission to make sure every moment is a little bit better than the last. Cheers.
